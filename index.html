The Leaf House

The concrete canopies in the form of overlapping but distinct leaves are supported by angled steel columns. The leaves are bent in multiple axes to give every pod a three dimensional feel. This house of gentle but sloping leaves is a comfortable, serene home of 650 sqm deeply rooted in nature. To add to its glory, The Leaf House was featured as a cover of Architectural Digest 2013. Also, this was featured in the October 2013 issue of Dezeen.
